Our Approach

At Matxo Cleaning Ltd., our approach is simple — professionalism, reliability, and eco-conscious service. We tailor our cleaning solutions to meet your unique needs, whether residential, commercial, or industrial.

Our skilled team uses modern tools and safe, effective products to ensure spotless results. We value trust, transparency, and consistent quality in every job.

Your space is more than just a place — it’s a reflection of you. Let us help you keep it fresh, clean, and welcoming.

Facade/High Level Window Cleaning

We provide window cleaning services for both internal and external glass at both hand height and high level, using our experienced window cleaners and professional equipment. Our services include cleaning windows, glass lifts, glass doors, glass roofs and canopies and other glass surfaces.

Hydro-jet Drain Cleaning

Hydro-jetting is one of the safest and most effective ways to clean the drain. The benefits of hydro jet cleaning is that it's environmentally friendly. No chemicals are used that might end up in the environment or, worse, damage your pipes.

Solar Panel Cleaning and Maintenance

MCSC cleans solar panels like no other company, offering state-of-the-art automatic systems that configure for your specific soiling environment and feature security fasteners and other anti-theft devices. Our products are available for both residential and commercial use.

Ventilation Cleaning Services

Our certified technicians meticulously inspect, calibrate and balance ventilation systems. Regular maintenance safeguards occupant health, regulatory compliance and building efficiency.
